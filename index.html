📊 Data Source & Methodology

International Monetary Fund (IMF) World Economic Outlook Database - PPP Exchange Rates (April 2025)

💡 How PPP works?

PPP rates show how much local currency you need to buy the same goods and services as $1 USD can buy in the United States. Higher PPP = more expensive country. Our salary calculator uses these rates to show equivalent purchasing power.

❓ How to Use This Tool

🏠

Step 1: Choose Your Country

Select the country where you currently live or earn your salary. This will be your reference point.

🌍

Step 2: Pick Comparison Country

Choose the country you want to compare living costs with. You can also click on the chart bars above!

💰

Step 3: Enter Your Salary

Type your monthly salary in your home country's currency. The tool will show you the equivalent amount needed in the other country.

📊

Step 4: Understand Results

The comparison shows how much more or less expensive the other country is, and what salary you'd need there for the same lifestyle.

💡 Example

If you earn $5,000/month in the USA and want to move to India, you'd only need about $1,200/month to maintain the same purchasing power because India is much cheaper!
